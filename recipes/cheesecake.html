Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecake is a cake made out of cheese. While there are numerous variations worldwide, this one in particular is that of the famous Basque Burnt Cheesecake. Here's the recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 900 gm of cream cheese
  • 200 gm of sugar
  • 5 large eggs
  • 340 ml of heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (390°F).
  2. Line a 9-inch cake pan with parchment paper.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  4. Add the eggs one by one, mixing well after each addition.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream, flour, vanilla extract, and salt.
  6. Pour the batter into the cake pan.
  7. Give it a couple tap to remove any potential air pockets.
  8. Bake for 50 minutes or until the top is dark brown.
  9. Let it cool before serving.
